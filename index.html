--- layout: default title: Grand Tour Project ---

The Grand Tour Explorer is a digital transformation of John Ingamells’ A Dictionary of British and Irish Travellers in Italy 1701-1800 (published for the Paul Mellon Centre for Studies in British Art by Yale University Press, 1997). It transforms this text into a research tool that facilitates multidimensional searching and browsing of the entries in Ingamells’ Dictionary; the organization of this information into structured and usable data; and the analysis and visualization of this data.
